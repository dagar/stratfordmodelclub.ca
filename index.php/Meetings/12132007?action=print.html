Date: Dec. 13 2007 Time Start: 7:35 No. Present; 19
President’s Comments: We intend to make this part of the meeting as short as possible in order to get on with the real business of the evening.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Stan Boyd Seconded; Mike Buhlman

Old Business:
New Business:
Guests/Entertainment: Members’ wives were present, making the evening far more pleasant than usual. Ted showed the 1/5 scale RV4 (not RA4 as was quoted in the last minutes) which, it is intended, will be presented to Chris Shean at the January meeting.

Adjournment at: 7:50 in order that the main event could commence. Congratulations and thanks to Ted and Elizabeth for organising the refreshments and prizes for the game that followed.
