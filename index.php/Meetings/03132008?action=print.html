STRATFORD MODEL CLUB

Minutes of meeting.

Date: Mar. 13, 2008 Time Start: 7:30 No. Present; 14
President’s Comments: Pleased to see all those present.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Steve Weadick Seconded; Mike Lantz
Correspondence: Phone call from John Bakker advising that Stratford Flying Club hopes to organise a tour of the Diamond Aircraft plant in London on Wed. April 16, p.m. time to be arranged. Those interested added their names to a list to be forwarded to the Flying club.
Treasurer’s Report. $2309.16 in bank, field paid.
Librarian’s Report: DVD and simulator returned. Nothing outstanding.
Old Business: Club membership application form now on the web site.
New Business:
Guests/Entertainment: Dave and Carlyle showed three examples of ALIGN T-Rex 450 helicopters, incredible value at around $450 less gyro and radio (gyro = approx. $150). Recommended for beginners since they are available with less expensive rotors and other equipment selling for about $250. A very nice aluminium carrying case is available which holds all you need at the field including spare batteries. Mike Buhlman showed his E-Flite Mini Funtana (ARF he says he built from scratch in two weeks!) Ted showed the bare bones of his Beaver fuselage, looking good. He was advised to coat the structure with dope or similar to keep the water out for his intended float operation.
50/50 Draw Winner: Steve Weadick got $16.00
Adjournment at: 8:32 Moved by: Bob Gladding Seconded by: Pete Skells.
