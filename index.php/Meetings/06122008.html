STRATFORD MODEL CLUB

Minutes of meeting.

Date: June 12, 2008 Time Start: 7:35 No. Present; 18
President’s Comments: Welcome all to our last meeting for this year.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Pete Skells Seconded: Bob Gladding
Correspondence: Ted and Murray received notification of New Hamburg’s fun fly: date June 8th.
Treasurer’s Report. $2426.59 plus some to go in.
Librarian’s Report: 1 book and 3 DVDs out.
Old Business: Fun fly prizes: Flightcraft has offered to match our purchase of gifts. It was decided that we should buy gift certificates to a value of $100. Stan moved and Mike Buhlman seconded that we make that $200. Mike Lantz then donated $100 for more prizes.
New Business: Chris Buhlman will organise the painting of the shed. Ted will have the paint tomorrow (June 13th.)
It was proposed that we obtain and use a “Fees Paid” receipt book. Stan moved to adopt the proposal and Ted seconded, all were in agreement.
Brad Forbeck is in a London hospital receiving a hip replacement, we wish him well. Vince is still having a hard time and we wish him well also.
Guests/Entertainment: David showed his E Flite Blade CX2. This impressive little chopper comes with radio, Li-pol battery and charger, costing less than $200 Cdn. Intended for indoor (or zero wind) flying, the radio is range limited and is suitable for this helicopter only. Stan Boyd showed a neat little electric biplane which he said took about 15 hours to build, the wing ribs occupying most of this.
50/50 Draw Winner: Trevor Scott won $18.00 which he promptly donated for gifts at the fun fly.
Adjournment at: 8:39 Moved: Bob Gladding seconded: Bill Strawbridge
