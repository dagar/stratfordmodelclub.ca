STRATFORD MODEL CLUB

Minutes of meeting.

Date: Sept. 11’08 Time Start: 7:42 No. Present; 18
President’s Comments: Welcome to all at this first meeting of the fall season.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Carlisle Anderson. Seconded: Bob Gladding.
Correspondence: Ted received an email from Brent Van Moosel enquiring about helicopter flyers in the club. Andrew Hancock was golfing at the Country Club, picture in local paper, and has bought a used Sportster.
Treasurer’s Report. $3584.60 in bank. An all time record of 41 paid up members, 7 new members this year.
Librarian’s Report: 1DVD out. Bill Strawbridge donated “Color Treasury of Model Airplanes”to the library.
Old Business: Many thanks to Chris Buhlman for painting the shed.
New Business: Thanks to Murray for his efforts in field maintenance all year. London Swap Meet date is Sunday October 12’08. We should fix our fun-fly date ASAP in order to avoid being ‘bumped’ as we were last year, all agreed we should do it NOW! The date would be; Sunday June 28 2009. We should nominate a vice president at the next meeting. The rules governing RC model flying need to be updated to accommodate changes in the nature of the type and performance of present models. MAAC rules are not at all well defined and confusion results. David Carey gave his well informed input regarding what is, and is not, in the MAAC rules. He is still awaiting some kind of response from MAAC and Bryan Hewitt since the unfortunate and wrongly placed criticism handed out at our fun-fly by Bryan, the Zone Director, for our ‘failure to comply with safety rules.’
Vince expressed his gratitude for the support offered by the club during his time off sick.
Guests/Entertainment: Ted showed his scratch built Four Star Sixty.
50/50 Draw Winner: Carlisle Anderson won a jug of fuel, the club collected $32.00.
Adjournment at: 8:50 Moved: Pete Skells Seconded: Chris Buhlman.
