STRATFORD MODEL CLUB

Minutes of meeting.

Date: Nov. 8 2007 Time Start: 7:32 No. Present; 16
President’s Comments: Ted was missing when the meeting started, he had a ‘False Alarm’ call to the emergency for a suspected high potassium following blood tests earlier in the day. He arrived a little late but just as cheerful as usual.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Stan Seconded; Bob
Correspondence: The club was asked if we will be building planes at the school again this year.
Murray says he has not received certification of insurance from MAAC and the Kiwanis centre needs proof of this insurance. MAAC wants to charge $25.00 for a certificate!
Treasurer’s Report. $2998.18 in the bank.
Librarian’s Report: One simulator and one DVD out.
Old Business: Ted is building a 1/5 scale RA4 for Chris Shean to thank him for his demonstrations at our fun flys. The total cost is likely to be $320.00. Vince reports that indoor flying is available in various locations in London.
New Business: We gave Pete a fruit basket and received a ‘thank you’ card from him. Murray proposed that we return the dues paid by Daniel Agar as a ‘thank you’ gesture for the work he has done on our website. All were in agreement. Ted proposed and Chris Buhlman seconded that the next meeting should be our Xmas ‘Bash’. Ted and Ron will set up a short list of members to receive minutes of meetings where their travelling time to attend is an obstacle. Any member that we are aware of who is sick or otherwise unable to attend, will be added to this list for as long as their problem exists.
Guests/Entertainment: Stan Boyd showed his electric indoor flyer,Gilmore Wendal Williams racer and Ron showed his 1.6 lb electric Sig Wonder, neither of which have yet flown.
50/50 Draw Winner: Murray wins $15.00.
Adjournment at: 8:23pm moved by Vince, seconded by Mike Lantz.
