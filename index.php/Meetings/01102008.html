STRATFORD MODEL CLUB

Minutes of meeting.

Date: Jan 10 2008 Time Start: 7:30 No. Present; 16
President’s Comments: Ted welcomed the guests.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Mike Buhlman Seconded; Pete Skells
Correspondence: Ted received an email from Winnipeg with a query on his Chipmunk.
Treasurer’s Report. $2824.65. All bills paid except the field.
Librarian’s Report: No report, librarian is away.
Old Business: Ted will check out the availability of insurance certificates from the MAAC web site.
New Business: Ted proposed June 29 for our ’08 fun fly. Moved ;Ted, 2nd Mike Buhlman. Looking for volunteers for the chores. There will be no rain date. Mike Buhlman offered to take care of the raffle of the plane for the fun fly and Carlyle offered to assist Mike. Mike Lantz offered to supply the motor. The possibility of a Mall show was discussed and the decision was that the interest was not there. So no show. Mike Lantz suggested a donation to a Stratford charity. Winter projects in progress were discussed and Vince showed his indoor micro plane.
Guests/Entertainment: Chris and wife Patti Shean were on hand to receive the 1/5 scale RV4 presented in appreciation of his aerobatic displays at several of our past fun flys, which were appreciated by members and visitors.
50/50 Draw Winner: Matt Quinn received $14.00
Adjournment at: 8:30 pm Moved by Vince and seconded by Ted.
