STRATFORD MODEL CLUB

Minutes of meeting.

Date: Feb 8 2008 Time Start: 7:30 No. Present; 10
President’s Comments: Ted welcomed those present.
Minutes read, Corrected and Accepted: Moved: Mike Buhlman Seconded; Pete Skells
Correspondence: Brian Hewitt emailed Ted saying that Otterville already had announced June 29th. as their fun fly date and as a result our date is revised to June 22nd. Murray had a call from the school requesting a repeat of the plane building demonstration. It will take place on Feb 29th.with Ted, Bob, Vince, Murray and Carlyle doing the honours.
Treasurer’s Report. $2918.65 in the bank. Field yet to be paid.
Librarian’s Report: 1 DVD and 1 simulator out.
Old Business: Mike and Chris Buhlman, Carlyle and Dave Carey agreed to set up the fun-fly equipment for June 22nd. Ted was able to download three copies of the Certificate of Insurance from the MAAC website and has sent them to Murray.
New Business: Carlyle would like to organise a get-together for helicopter flyers for a Saturday, the date yet to be decided. Chris Buhlman seconded, all in favour. Carlyle will phone his contacts when the date is decided.
Guests/Entertainment: Ted had the 1/2 wing for his Beaver which he intends to fit with floats and detachable wheels. To reduce the risk of water entering the wing through push-rod openings, he has kept flap and aileron actuators entirely enclosed within the wing. Current projects were discussed, Chris Buhlman showed his newly acquired Hangar 9 kit for an Extra 260, partly finished and a good deal for $400 Cdn. Dave Carey had his scratch built mould and an example helicopter canopy. A very professional looking product.
50/50 Draw Winner: Ron Wyman got $10.00
Adjournment at: 8:30 pm Pete Skells, seconded, Bob Gladding.
