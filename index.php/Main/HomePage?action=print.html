Who We Are

The Stratford Model Club consists of a membership of 40 plus with approximately 20 active flying members.The club exists for the sole purpose of the enjoyment of all types of R/C modelling but focuses mainly on R/C aircraft. The Stratford Model Club was formed more than twenty years ago and continues today to be quite active.

How to Join

If you're not already a MAAC member, fill out the MAAC Member Application form, then the Stratford Model Club Membership form. Submit both to either Stephen Weadick or Murray Zulauf.

Club Meetings

Meetings are held the second Thursday of every month September - June. Information from past meetings can be found here.
